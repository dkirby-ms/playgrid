## Key Decision

GITHUB_TOKEN prevents workflow recursion by design. The `[skip ci]` directive was redundant, dangerous in squash-merge contexts, and causing silent deployment skips.

**Learning:** Never use `[skip ci]` in commit messages when workflows involve squash-merging.
