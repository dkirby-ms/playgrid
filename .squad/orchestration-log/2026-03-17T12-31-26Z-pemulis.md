### Risk Reinforcement Calculation Bug Fix

**Issue:** RiskPlugin calculated reinforcements for the wrong player during turn transitions.

**Root Cause:** `endPhase` handler computed reinforcements before returning `endsTurn: true`. Since `BaseGameRoom.advanceTurn()` processes `endsTurn` immediately, the turn advanced to the next player before reinforcements were finalized.

**Solution:** Introduced `onTurnStarted` lifecycle hook to `IGamePlugin` interface.

- Added hook to `GameLifecycle` in `shared/src/gamePlugin.ts`
- Implemented hook call in `BaseGameRoom.advanceTurn()` after turn advances
- Implemented hook in `RiskPlugin` to calculate reinforcements, reset `turnPhase`, and clear `earnedCardThisTurn`
- Removed redundant reinforcement calculation from `endPhase`
- Added 4 regression tests to `server/src/__tests__/risk.test.ts`

**Impact:**
- Risk now correctly awards reinforcements to the active player
- Other plugins (Checkers, Backgammon) unaffected — hook is optional
- Pattern established for future per-turn initialization (card dealing, timer resets, etc.)
